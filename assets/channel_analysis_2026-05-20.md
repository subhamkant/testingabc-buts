## Key Findings (the part that matters for Phase 1+)

1. **Channel is in early ramp, not plateau.** 18 days old, +28 subs net, 91% of views from algorithm-driven Shorts feed. The algorithm is doing its job; the bottleneck is *what gets surfaced*.

2. **The `महाभारत #N:` prefix is the single biggest reach killer.** Three of the 5 worst recent performers carry it (0, 4, 31 views). The top 3 organic performers (861, 549, 358 views) do NOT carry it. Same channel, same audience, same production quality — the only differentiator on the worst videos is the numbered prefix. **Phase 1 is empirically validated as the highest-priority change.**

3. **Retention is already good.** 61.7% channel-wide AVP; top videos at 67–75%. The hook validator and beat-spacing work (Phases 2 / 8) will *improve* it but isn't the urgent bottleneck.

4. **Engagement is the structural weak point.** 11 comments across 31 videos. Phases 4 (pinned-comment debate-bait), 9 (in-video provocative line), 21 (quotable lines), 22 (community formation) address this.

5. **Long-form is invisible.** 17 long-form uploads but near-zero watch from non-Shorts traffic sources. The Shorts→Long-form funnel (Phase 7) is missing; long-form is currently dead inventory. Separate workstream.

6. **Search is only 3% of traffic.** Tag long-tail work (Phase 3) is worth doing but won't move the needle alone. The Shorts feed surfaces by topic + early-engagement signals, not by tags — so Phase 9 / 22 / 17 move the needle more than Phase 3.

7. **Suggested-video traffic is 0.08%.** Videos aren't compounding off each other. Phase 17 cliffhanger architecture + Phase 13 identity signature are the levers for this.

8. **80/7.5/12.5 country split (IN/US/diaspora).** The "keep bilingual" decision is correct — but tone, vocabulary, and audience-persona (Phase 18) should anchor on the Indian Hindi audience.

## Measurement Plan

Re-run `analyze_channel.py` weekly. Track the following deltas against this baseline:

- **Last-7-vs-prior-7 trend** — expect to flip from −33.8% to positive within 14 days of Phase 1 shipping (title prefix removed).
- **Average view % across all videos** — current 61.7%, target ≥70% after Phases 2 + 8 + 10 ship.
- **Comments per video** — current 0.35, target ≥2.0 after Phases 4 + 9 + 21 + 22 ship.
- **Returning-viewer rate** — not yet measurable at 18 days; will become measurable around day 30. **This is the north-star metric per the plan.**
- **Suggested-video traffic share** — current 0.08%, target ≥5% by day 90 (Phase 17 + 13 + 19 lever).
- **Top-N video AVP gap** — currently the top 3 are at 67–72% AVP, the middle of the pack at 30–55%. Phases 8 + 14 should compress this gap upward.
